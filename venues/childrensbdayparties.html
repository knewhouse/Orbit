CHILDREN'S BIRTHDAY PARTIES


Start Line Club Birthday Package

World of Speed Museum in the Learning Lane or Classroom

The Birthday Package Includes:

  • Reserved party room for two hours — Learning Lane or Classroom
  • Museum Main Gallery access, including the Start Line Club, for up to 35 people. Start Line Club will remain open to public. Additional museum access available for $9 per person.
  • Black and white checkered flag tablecloths, paper plates, cups, napkins, and utensils
  • Plastic racing helmet for the birthday child, and additional helmets available for purchase
  • Additional décor available

Extra Party Activities:

  • Racing Simulator Slots | $10 per ride. Time slots must be reserved and paid for in advance.
  • Pinewood Derby | $100 per hour
  • Radio Control Race Track | $900 per event
  • Radio Control Race Track | $900 per event
  • Guided Museum Tour or Scavenger Hunt available at no additional cost. Must be reserved in advance.

Cost: $200

You may bring a commercially prepared cake or cupcakes. If you would like to bring in additional food, we can provide you with an approved vendors list.
